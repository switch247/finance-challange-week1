Interim Submission Guideline

The interim submission will provide a structured overview of the challenge, including its core objectives and project goals, followed by a detailed step-by-step breakdown of the project implementation process. It will cover the tasks completed so far, the methods and tools used, and the impact of these tasks on the project, supported by visual representations where necessary. Additionally, the submission will update the status of ongoing tasks and outline the next steps, ensuring a clear roadmap for continued project progress.
Feel free to include any additional information that you believe will enhance the submission.





Final Report Submission Guideline

The final submission will provide a comprehensive overview of the completed project, summarizing the implementation process, key steps taken, challenges faced, and lessons learned. It will outline the general methodologies and tools used, present key findings with visual representations to demonstrate their relation to the initial objectives, and conclude with a summary of outcomes, acknowledgment of limitations, and suggestions for future improvements and directions.

Feel free to include any additional information or visual representations that you believe will enhance the submission and provide a clear, thorough understanding of the project's entirety.
